Overview

Usada Pekora is a 111 year old Moon Rabbit, who joined Hololive as a member of the Hololive Fantasy (3rd) generation. She debuted on July 17, 2019. To make her characterization of a rabbit stand out, there are carrots in her braids to hold them in place. She is known for her chaotic personality and her laugh AH↗️HA↘️HA↗️HA↘️HA↗️, sometimes called the Jungle laugh by fans. Because of this she has become one of the most recognized members.

Personality

Pekora is known for being very chaotic and a prankster, someone who likes to play by her own rules. This is evidenced by her various antics in Minecraft.She has a unique verbal tic in which she ends her sentences with the "ぺこ(peko)" suffix. Here the language barrier prevents any kind of a translation. We end our sentences with a period for writing and a pause in conversation. This just makes her even more unique and recognizable. Pekora is quick to react anger when things go awry. She tends to run away when she faces a powerful foes in video games, apologizes meekly until she finds an opening or when the situation changes favorably, and then starts talking down to them.
