Overview

Kiryu Coco debuted on December 28, 2019. She is a 3,500 year old seven meter tall dragon. She joined Hololive to pay for the CEO's hospital bill after she crushed him by accident. Unlike Haato, Coco is completely bilingual, and is the only member of Hololive Japan that speaks both Japanese and English fluently. In the time before Hololive EN, Coco was probably where most of English speakers went to. In fact, Coco has a very western sense of humor, makng it easier to joke around and entertain. She has parodied the Tim and Eric Awesome Show in a skit during her AsaCoco morning show, taken inspiration from RuPaul's Drag Race when coming up with the background for her debut, and admitting to be a fan of Shakira. Coco is my personal favorite to watch. The English really helps with that.

Personality

Coco is usually very loud and energetic, and often tries to push the boundaries of what she can try on stream, at the dismay of the staff. Here boundary pushing antics include, but are not limited to monthly hair removal streams, discussing real-time attack (RTA) techniques for toilet speedrunning, and teaching the Japanese memebers and viewers Internet English. Coco is a content gold mine, but she also has a more mature side. She shares advice on different serious topics with her viewers and even her colleagues. Finally, she is very ambitious and hard-working. It is not surprising to see her stream more than once a day. During the runtime of the famed AsaCoco morning show, it is well-known that Coco worked late into the night until the early hours of the morning

Asacoco

Asacoco comes from 朝(asa), meaning morning, and appending her name to the end of it. Makes sense, it's a morning show she runs. It's a short 30~ minute-ish program about small ongoings and events going on in Hololive. It premeried the day after her debut and continued to be streamed every weekday since then until April 18, 2020 when Coco received a live-streaming ban. Her boundary pushing finally got a little too out of hand for YouTube. On June 7, 2020, AsaCoco Version 2 was launched. AsaCoco would begin to integrate English lessons on slang or casual phrases for its Japanese audience.On July 7 2020, Asacoco went on an extended break as Coco needed time to handle real life affairs. Due to the long hours she puts into the show and real life, Asacoco has stayed on an extended break
