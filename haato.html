Haato

Akai Haato, literally meaning red heart, debuted on June 2, 2018. As a member of the 1st first generation, Haato is one of the oldest members of Hololive. Haato's character is a schoolgirl studying abroad. In fact, she recently graduated from high school in Australia. This relates to how Haato stands out; she knows some English. For a long time, Haato was the only way the English speaking world interacted with Hololive. Haato's personality is tsundere. She displays moments of superiority and contrarianism mixed in with humbleness and openess. This isn't the whole story

Haachama

Albert Einstein once said the definition of insanity is “doing the same thing over and over again and expecting different results." He clearly never met Haachama. While in Australia, Haato underwent a change and turned into Haachama. The first emergence of Haachama can be traced back to a Getting Over It with Bennett Foddy playthrough. She played with her feet (giving up mid-way), paused for a moment to take a whiff of their scent and later stated that she enjoys sniffing them.
